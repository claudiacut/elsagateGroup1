Subliminal messages

How Peppa Pig's show has been distorted


Little kids tend to recognize their favourite characters in the thumbnails and click on them, binge-watching those videos showcasing a very familiar imagery. Peppa Pig is one of the most exploited characters, most probably because of the simplicity of its 2d drawings (it is quite easy to manipulate the characters) and because of the audience: kids under 4 tend to just watch one episode after the other.
The hidden danger is therefore very subtle; often the inappropriate content result just in a small portion of the entire video: many times in fact, scary frames or violent actions appear for a few seconds, without causing an entire distortion of the whole video, preventing, this way, its reporting and the consequential removal from the net.
