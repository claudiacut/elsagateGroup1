PEPPA PIG

when the problem is more subtle


Little kids tend to recognize their favourite characters in the thumbnails appearing on the net and click on them, binge-watching those videos showcasing familiar imagery. Peppa Pig, being a huge success all over the world, is one of the most exploited characters. Due to the simplicity of its 2d drawings, it is quite easy to manipulate the characters and the general plot of the episode. The distortion that occurs works because of the audience: kids under 4 tend to just watch one episode after the other, not really focusing on the footage development or on the plot itself: they are attracted by shapes, colors and sounds.




The hidden danger is therefore very subtle; often the inappropriate content result just in a small portion of the entire video: many times in fact, scary frames or violent actions appear for a few seconds, just like a flash, without causing an entire distortion of the whole video, preventing, this way, its reporting and the consequential removal from the net.
Many times instead, Peppa pig is deliberately used to convey explicit and violent messages, creators behind these contents associate innocent characters with extreme savagery: the popularity of Peppa, along with violent and obscene images, works just perfectly to earn views.
